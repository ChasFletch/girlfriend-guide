Know the Enemy 👀

San Diego FC — they were THAT team last year. 2026 has been deeply humbling. Six matches without a win. 9 players out, a 22-year-old keeper, and a defender (Chris McVey) who's been red-carded in 3 of his last 4 starts. Captain Jeppe Tverskov has had enough: "It needs to stop." Coach Varas: "I can't explain what's happening." They also have a former Dynamo Homegrown — Kieran Sargeant — who Houston traded away for a third-round draft pick. He's out injured tonight so Houston doesn't have to feel too bad about that one. And yes: last time these two met at Shell Energy (Oct 4, 2025), SDFC won 4-2. Tonight is different.
